ACADEMIC ENVIRONMENT AT ZEN:

  1. The environment at ZEN provides children with opportunities and incentives to learn. Children are encouraged to develop their individual talents and assume their roles in life and society with confidence, courage and determination.

  2. The curriculum at ZEN is systematic, value based, practical and skill focused. The school is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education and enriches its learning style, by adopting a curriculum that is latest in the field of Education.

  3. At ZEN, we understand the children and have designed specifically to ensure that our children not only get the best of education but also explore and develop their hidden talents. Both curricular and co-curricular activities are given equal importance for the all round developments of the children.
