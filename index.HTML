ZEN laid its strong foundations of learning at PADRA, Vadodara in 1998 and established itself through its unique and rich holistic approach towards the teaching learning process by integrating the traditional learning methods with a blend of the modern techno-savvy aids with an aim to enable the child to explore and experience greater heights in learning.

 ZEN came up with a new unit in the year 2007 at Vallabh Vidhyanagar.

 The world is an oyster for ZEN students. Here, we continuously endeavour to make the educational experience rich and sound for all our learners. ZEN operates a state-of-art school system balancing traditional academic rigours with modern day ideas.

 ZEN is committed to partner with parents, teachers and community members to develop confident, responsible, compassionate, creative, cooperative and competent students as global citizens.
